## 2024-05-12 - Strategy Selection Optimization (HybridStrategy)
**Learning:** Cascading `.map().filter()` array manipulations on hot paths (like candidate strategy generation in `HybridStrategy`) generates massive numbers of intermediate arrays and puts high pressure on the Node.js GC. Finding the maximum via mapped arrays and O(N log N) `.sort()` is unnecessarily expensive compared to keeping track of the best candidate in O(N).
**Action:** When filtering objects into disjoint subset levels, iterate over the objects array precisely once and construct the specific resulting structures conditionally within the iteration block. To select maximum/minimums concurrently, hoist external system calls (like `Date.now()`) outside the tracking loop to avoid redundant evaluations inside the iteration.
