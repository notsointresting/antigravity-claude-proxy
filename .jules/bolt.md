## 2024-05-14 - Optimize array processing in strategy candidates selection
**Learning:** During account selection strategies (like HybridStrategy), multiple filters (`.map().filter()`) are applied on arrays of accounts to calculate candidates for normal use, fallback, emergency, and last resort. When doing this sequentially, intermediate `{ account, index }` wrapper arrays are allocated and discarded multiple times, putting unnecessary GC pressure on a hot path.
**Action:** Instead of chaining `.map().filter()` or running separate iterations for different fallback tiers, use a single-pass `for` loop to filter and categorize items into specific arrays (e.g., `candidates`, `fallback`, `emergency`, `lastResort`). This executes the logic with minimal intermediate allocations and avoids the performance overhead of recreating wrapper objects dynamically.
